Growing Up in Maine

I grew up not far from Acadia National Park...

How to catch the best breakfast:

  1. Wait for a rainy night; wake up early, check the tide to see if it is high enough
  2. If the tide is high, grab your fishing gear
  3. Go to the small bridge by my grandmother's farm
  4. Cast, wait until a fish is hooked
  5. Reel in your breakfast
  6. Remove fish from hook, put on ice
  7. Transfer fish home where you can grill it up!
